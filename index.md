# Quantum Error Mitigation with Zero-Noise Extrapolation

Welcome to my project exploring **Quantum Error Mitigation (QEM)** techniques for Noisy Intermediate-Scale Quantum (NISQ) devices.

This project investigates the effectiveness of **Zero-Noise Extrapolation (ZNE)** under different quantum noise models and compares the performance of **First-Order** and **Second-Order Richardson Extrapolation**.

---

## Project Overview

Current quantum computers are highly susceptible to noise, which limits the accuracy of quantum computations. While Quantum Error Correction remains the long-term solution, its large resource requirements make it impractical for today's NISQ devices.

This project focuses on **Zero-Noise Extrapolation (ZNE)** as a practical alternative for mitigating noise without requiring additional qubits.
